I have always said that my only ambition and passion in life is knowledge...

...and the journey that leads to it.

So with a relentless search for challenges and the ambition to gain as much knowledge as possible on as many topics as possible, I decided to embark on the adventure of becoming a web developer. Some years ago something started itching in the back of my mind..."you can't miss the opportunity to make your mark in this new technological era that we are living in", since I see this technological revolution as the new era of explorers, and combining that with my passion for everything that has to do with a computer, I could not pass up the opportunity. More than a year and a half ago I finally decided to start training and forming myself to become one of the people who will shape web 3.0.
